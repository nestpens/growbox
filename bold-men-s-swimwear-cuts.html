What Makes a Cut Bold?

"Bold" doesn’t always mean revealing—though it certainly can. Sometimes, boldness lies in subtlety: the way a seam lifts, the confidence of a contoured pouch, or the choice to wear a bikini instead of boardshorts.

Here’s what sets bold men’s swimwear cuts apart:

  • Intentional silhouette design that lifts, shapes, or reveals
  • Low-rise or cutaway designs that challenge tradition
  • Sheer, mesh, or strapless elements that defy convention
  • Backless swimwear styles that blur the line between fashion and fetish
  • Compression or support features that act like shapewear without restriction
